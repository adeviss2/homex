The Bravo apartments are situated in the Salamanca district, between the metro stations of Manuel Becerra and Ventas, in a centrally-located residential area of Madrid offering great connections to all other parts of the city. A traditional, quiet neighbourhood with a great variety of bars, restaurants and shops of all kinds. Close to Plaza de las Ventas and the entry to the M-30 motorway.

Just a few steps away from the apartment, guests can enjoy the park of Eva Perón, a beautiful green area with sports facilities right in the heart of Madrid.

The apartment:

With 3 bedrooms, 2 full bathrooms and a capacity of 7 people, this apartment is the perfect choice for staying in Madrid: high quality accommodation with well laid-out spaces and an exclusive interior design at a very competitive price.

The apartment is located on the ground floor and has access to a bright interior courtyard.

It comes with a master bedroom with a double bed and an en-suite bathroom with shower, a twin room, a single room and a double sofa bed in the living room.

Its exquisite, colourful home décor inspired by the latest trends is sure to provide a pleasant stay in Madrid.

All Bravo apartments are equipped down to the smallest detail: they all come with hot/cold air conditioning in every room, mixer taps, gas boiler, washing machine and dishwasher, kitchen appliances, bed linen, towels and Wifi connection.Not allowed: Parties, Animals/Pets
