Two Bedroom Apartment, Sleeps 6

Beautiful apartament in the most exclusive area of Madrid, in front of Retiro park and in the middle of the ‘Milla de Oro’.

Lagasca II is an exclusive apartamet in a new building located in one of the most classical and historical areas of Madrid.

On the ground floor of this duplex apartment you will find a complete bathroom, fully equipped kitchen and a beautiful design living room with a large window facing the street Lagasca and San Manuel and San Benito church. The sofa can be used as a double bed (1,40 x 1,90 m)

On the next floor there is another independent bathroom and two bedrooms, one of them with double bed of 1,50 x 1,20 m and the other one with two single beds of 0,90 x 2,00 m.

Lagasca street is located in the ‘Milla de Oro’ in Salamanca neighbourhood, possibly the most exclusive area of Madrid, nearby the Museums of Puerta de Alcalá, the Town Hall, Retiro Park, and the most interesting attractions to visit.

Besides close to the apartment can be found the best shops and brands, best restaurants, bars and some supermarkets.
