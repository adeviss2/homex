Licence number: VT-4742
This apartment is located in the Salamanca district, one of the safest and most sought-after areas in the centre of Madrid, just opposite the famous Las Ventas bullring and close to all major tourist attractions in the city. The luxury stores of the “Milla de Oro” shopping area are also within easy walking distance. An ideal apartment for both short and long-term visits, for business and pleasure alike.

The apartment:

A newly-built apartment (2017) in a refurbished building: brand new!
This apartment is located on the 1st floor of a building with 2 elevators and can comfortably accommodate groups of up to 9 people. Smartly designed and equipped to make the most of space, it features top-quality furniture and all the modern conveniences that guests may require throughout their stay.
The living/dining area comes with a single sofa bed, a pull-down double bed (160 x 200 cm), 2 dining tables that seat 10 and a large breakfast bar with 3 stools. The integrated kitchen is fully equipped with everything you may need to enjoy a comfortable, hassle-free stay.
The sleeping area comprises 2 bedrooms: a double with a double bed and a room provided with 2 bunk beds that turn into a handy desk. All beds are equipped with memory foam mattress (28 cm).
The apartment has 2 modern, comfortable bathrooms (one of which is en-suite), both provided with a large shower with thermostatic mixer, 500 litres hot water storage and heated towel rails.
